TEDxYouth@JNS
We are thrilled to announce the first edition of TEDxYouth@JNS! Created in the spirit of TED’s mission- “ideas worth spreading”, TEDxYouth@JNS aims to provide eye-opening, intellect-stimulating and conversation-sparking experiences that will broaden your mind and inspire you to ‘Be The Change’. It symbolises the dissemination of unconventional, path breaking ideas; stirring intellection and imaginative thinking. TEDxYouth@JNS is going to be held on the 15th of December, 2018 at Jamnabai Narsee School . We aim to have a plethora of speakers from different walks of life in order to make the event a one of its kind enriching experience.
